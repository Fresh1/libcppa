template<scheduling_hint Hint>
actor_ptr cppa::spawn_in_group ( const group_ptr &  grp,
void_function  fun 
)

Spawns a new context-switching or thread-mapped actor that joins grp immediately.

Note:
The spawned actor joins grp after its init member function is called but before it is executed. Hence, the spawned actor already joined the group before this function returns.
